# PartPulse - App Name Proposals

Based on the requirements for an internal spare parts distribution management system for Trane Thermo King Pty LTD, here are five suitable name options:

## 1. PartPulse ‚≠ê (SELECTED)
**Rationale**: 
- "Part" clearly indicates the app's focus on spare parts
- "Pulse" suggests real-time monitoring and the heartbeat of operations
- Short, memorable, and easy to pronounce
- Professional yet modern sound
- Domain-friendly (.com, .io, .app likely available)

**Tagline**: "The heartbeat of your parts distribution"

---

## 2. TraneTrack
**Rationale**:
- Incorporates the company name (Trane)
- "Track" emphasizes tracking and monitoring functionality
- Clear association with the parent company
- Professional and corporate-sounding
- Easy to remember for internal use

**Tagline**: "Track every part, every time"

---

## 3. SpareSync
**Rationale**:
- "Spare" directly relates to spare parts
- "Sync" suggests synchronization and efficiency
- Modern, tech-forward sound
- Implies seamless integration
- Easy to spell and pronounce

**Tagline**: "Synchronize your spare parts workflow"

---

## 4. PartsHub
**Rationale**:
- "Hub" suggests a central point for all parts-related activities
- Simple and straightforward
- Easy to understand for all user levels
- Scalable name for future features
- Professional yet accessible

**Tagline**: "Your central parts management hub"

---

## 5. TechServe Parts
**Rationale**:
- "TechServe" combines technology and service
- Clearly indicates it's for parts management
- Professional corporate naming
- Aligns with service-oriented business
- Emphasizes both the technical and service aspects

**Tagline**: "Serving technicians, managing parts"

---

## Recommendation

**PartPulse** is recommended as the optimal choice because:

1. **Memorability**: Short, catchy, and easy to remember
2. **Clarity**: Clearly communicates the app's purpose
3. **Professionalism**: Suitable for corporate environment
4. **Scalability**: Works well as the app grows with additional features
5. **Branding**: Strong potential for logo and visual identity
6. **User Adoption**: Easy for both technical and non-technical users

The name "PartPulse" has been implemented throughout the application codebase, documentation, and user interface.
